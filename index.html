About Decco

Decco is an app that allows you to design and customize your home in a VR environment using furniture available in real-life stores. Decco allows you to visualize your ideas immediately without spending hundreds of dollars in physical prototypes.

The experience begins outlining the dimensions of the space. You can select, grab and throw furniture around from a 3D miniature catalogue of products. At the end of the experience you will have a blueprint of the space and a budget with prices of the products chosen for your new home.
